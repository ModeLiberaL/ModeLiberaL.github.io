Research Map

34 个官方主题 · 中文聚类

官方 CfP 列出 34 个研究主题。为便于导航，这里整理为 8 个主题簇，保留高频方向与 ACL 2026 特别主题。

01

LLM、Agent 与效率

  • Language Models / AI & LLM Agents
  • LLM Efficiency / Code Models
  • Retrieval-Augmented Language Models
02

可解释性与推理

  • 特别主题：Explainability of NLP Models
  • Interpretability and Model Analysis
  • Discourse, Pragmatics and Reasoning
  • Mathematical / Symbolic / Logical Reasoning
03

理解、检索与生成

  • Information Extraction and Retrieval
  • Natural Language Generation / Summarization
  • Dialogue / Question Answering
  • Semantics and Textual Inference
04

语言多样性与结构

  • Multilinguality and Language Diversity
  • Low-resource Methods / Machine Translation
  • Syntax, Parsing, Morphology and Phonology
05

多模态与语音

  • Multimodality and Language Grounding
  • Vision, Robotics and Beyond
  • Speech Processing and Spoken Language Understanding
06

可信与人本 NLP

  • Safety and Alignment in LLMs
  • Ethics, Bias, and Fairness
  • Human-AI Interaction and Cooperation
07

领域应用

  • Clinical and Biomedical Applications
  • Financial Applications and Time Series
  • Computational Social Science / NLP for Social Good
  • General NLP Applications
08

资源、评测与语言科学

  • Resources and Evaluation
  • Generalizability and Transfer
  • Linguistic Theories / Cognitive Modeling
  • Machine Learning for NLP
